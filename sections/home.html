The International Conference on Information Systems Security (ICISS), held annually in India, is a forum for dissemination of research results related to computer security. ICISS 2021, the 17th edition of the conference, will be held at IIT Patna from December 17 to 19, 2021. Over the past several years, the acceptance rate of ICISS has averaged less than 30%. The previous proceedings of the conference are published by Springer Verlag as part of their LNCS series. ICISS encourages submissions from academia, industry and government. Its scope covers both theoretical and applied research within the broad area of information and systems security. Papers must be written in English and submitted in the Springer Verlag LNCS Latex or Word style in PDF format, via this Submission link. The conference proceedings will appear in Springer's LNCS series.

Due to COVID-19, ICISS 2021 is planned to be VIRTUAL only, and encourages the online (remote) participation.
